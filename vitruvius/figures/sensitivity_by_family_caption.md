# Position sensitivity by architecture family (aggregated)

Mean position sensitivity averaged over (encoder, dataset) pairs within each architecture family, one bar per shuffle mode. Error bars are 1 standard deviation across the (encoder, dataset) pairs contributing to each family (transformer family has 3 encoders × 3 datasets = 9 points; each non-transformer family has 1 encoder × 3 datasets = 3 points).

This is the headline synthesis for the Phase 9 opinions section: does retrieval quality depend on sequential position, and if so, how much does it differ across the four architectural families?

Source: `experiments/phase8/<encoder>__<dataset>__<mode>.json`.
